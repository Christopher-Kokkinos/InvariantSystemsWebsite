Non-negotiables

Boundaries

Invariant Systems is diagnostic-only. Our output is a regime classification and a boundary map of safe inference. If an engagement requires optimisation, internals, or prediction, it is declined.

We do

  • Classify behavioural regimes from behavioural evidence
  • Identify invalid assumptions and where evaluation becomes unreliable
  • Specify what cannot be inferred under current system–environment coupling
  • Run bounded engagements with clear entry/exit

We do not

  • No optimisation (no tuning, no prompts, no parameter advice)
  • No consulting disguised as diagnosis
  • No model inspection (no internals, code, weights, training data)
  • No prediction of outcomes or performance
  • No compliance audit or certification activity

If you want “how to fix it”, we can still be useful by telling you which fixes are structurally impossible under current coupling, and which assumptions you must stop relying on.

Strategic invariants

  • Diagnostic primacy over optimisation
  • Behaviour over internals
  • Clarity over jargon
  • Boundaries over growth
  • Integrity over speed
