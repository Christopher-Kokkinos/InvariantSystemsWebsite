Eligibility check

How to engage

The first step is an eligibility check. If eligible, we proceed to the SSDA with a bounded evidence intake. If not, we will decline quickly and clearly.

Five eligibility questions

  1. Can you provide behavioural evidence (A–D)? Decision traces, outcomes/feedback, change logs, and lightweight context.
  2. Are you seeking a diagnosis, not a fix? The output is regime classification and boundaries, not optimisation.
  3. Can the engagement stay bounded? Short, contained scope with a clear exit.
  4. Is “no internals” acceptable? We do not require source code, training data, or model internals.
  5. Are you willing to accept an inferential-boundary outcome? Sometimes the correct result is that reliability cannot be validly inferred under current coupling and available behavioural evidence.
